# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 28, 2026  
**Effective Date:** April 28, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Oro** ("we," "us," or "our"), a parimutuel prediction market platform focused on Bhutanese archery events. Oro is operated as a Telegram Mini App and processes personal and financial data in connection with the services described below.

By accessing or using Oro, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the platform.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account & Identity Information
- **Bhutanese Citizen ID (CID):** Required for identity verification during account registration.
- **Telegram user data:** User ID, username, display name, and profile photo provided by Telegram upon authorization.

### 2.2 Financial Information
- **Account balances:** BTN (Bhutanese Ngultrum), USDT, and demo credit balances held on the platform.
- **Payment records:** Transaction amounts, payment methods (DK Bank / TON), timestamps, and status for all deposits and withdrawals.
- **DK Bank details:** Merchant transaction references and OTP confirmation data processed during DK Bank payments. We do not store your full bank credentials or PIN.
- **TON wallet addresses:** Public wallet addresses used for cryptocurrency deposits and withdrawals.

### 2.3 Betting & Activity Data
- **Bet history:** Markets you participated in, amounts staked, outcomes, and winnings.
- **Prediction market activity:** Positions held, contracts purchased, and settlement records.
- **Tournament participation:** Nominations, rounds, and archery event results relevant to your bets.

### 2.4 Technical Data
- **Session tokens:** JWT tokens used to authenticate your session (stored client-side).
- **Device & connection data:** IP address, browser/app version, and Telegram client information.
- **Usage logs:** Page views, interactions, and timestamps for platform activity.
- **WebSocket session data:** Real-time connection metadata for live market updates.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the following grounds:

- **Contractual necessity:** To provide the prediction market service, process bets, and settle payments.
- **Legal obligation:** To comply with applicable Bhutanese financial regulations and identity verification requirements.
- **Legitimate interests:** To detect fraud, maintain platform security, and improve our services.
- **Consent:** For any optional data collection beyond the above (you may withdraw consent at any time).

## 5. Data Sharing

We share data only in limited circumstances:

- **DK Bank (Druk PNB Bank):** Transaction data required to process BTN deposits and withdrawals via the bank's merchant API.
- **TON Blockchain:** Withdrawal transactions are broadcast to the public TON blockchain. Public wallet addresses and amounts are visible on-chain.
- **Telegram:** We receive data from Telegram under their platform terms; we do not send personal data back to Telegram beyond what their Mini App SDK requires.
- **Service providers:** Infrastructure and hosting providers who process data on our behalf under data processing agreements.
- **Legal authorities:** Where required by Bhutanese law or valid legal process.

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- **Encryption in transit:** All API communication uses HTTPS/TLS.
- **Password hashing:** Credentials are hashed using BCrypt before storage.
- **2FA (TOTP):** Admin access requires time-based one-time password authentication.
- **Bearer token authentication:** API requests require valid JWT tokens.
- **Redis session management:** Short-lived session data is stored securely server-side.
- **Access controls:** Administrative functions are restricted to verified admin accounts with audit logging of all admin actions.

No system is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

## 10. TON Cryptocurrency

Transactions on the TON blockchain are **public and immutable**. When you make a deposit or withdrawal using TON:

- Your wallet address and transaction amount are recorded on the public blockchain.
- We cannot delete or modify on-chain data.
- Connecting your TON wallet via TONConnect shares only your public wallet address with us.
